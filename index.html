Computer Graphics & Game Development, CG2D Cell is a community for digital creativity and interactive entertainment. We offer workshops, projects, and seminars on 3D modeling, animation, game design, and real-time rendering.

Slime Back In Time

A charming puzzle platformer where you play as a time-traveling jelly collecting ancient artifacts. The game features evolving landscapes that reveal hidden paths and a unique mechanic where players can use their previous "death points" as platforms. With its low-poly cartoonish visuals and cozy atmosphere, the game turns failure into opportunity while telling a story of digital archaeology.

Souls And Beyond

A puzzle game that takes you on an exciting trip through the rich and magical realm of Hindu mythology. Solve the secrets of time-reversal, relying on your brains and skill to conquer the obstacles that await you.

As you go through the divine landscape, you'll come across a slew of puzzles that shall force you to churn your brains. Souls and Beyond is a game that will take you beyond your imagination, with a gripping gameplay.
